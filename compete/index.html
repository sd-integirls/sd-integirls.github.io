Special thanks ...

Thank you to everyone who participated in the INTEGIRLS SD 2025 Spring Competition! Thanks to the hard work of the INTEGIRLS San Diego team and our generous sponsors, we were able to host over 100 girls and non-binary people from the area at our in-person event on 4/19.

It was remarkable to see the culmination of months of planning and preparation result in such a successful competition. We hope our competition was able to foster a feeling of community or belonging in every attendee, whether a first-time or seasoned competitor. We are incredibly proud of all participants for choosing to challenge themselves.

Special thank you to AoPS Gaithersburg, Wolfram, Akamai Foundation, Jane Street, SIG, AwesomeMath, and David Morin for sponsoring and donating prizes.

We hope to see everyone at our next competition!
